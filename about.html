🎯 What is PrepShot?

PrepShot is a free one-shot lecture platform designed for students preparing for competitive exams like JEE, NEET, and board examinations. We curate and organise the best free lectures from YouTube so you don't have to hunt for them — everything you need is in one place, structured by batch, subject, and chapter.

📚 What We Offer Today

  • One-shot lecture videos covering full chapters in a single session
  • Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and more
  • Organised by batch, subject, and chapter for easy navigation
  • Zero registration required — just open and start learning
  • Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop
